Why Hire a Web Designer Instead of DIY: 5 Benefits of Hiring a Professional

December 12, 2022·Jon Herrera 3 min read

Do you need a website? If so, you can either build it yourself or have someone do it for you. The problem with the first approach: unless you call yourself a professional web designer, getting a high-quality, fully responsive, and highly intuitive site is a far-fetched idea. On the other hand, you can hire a professional web designer and spend your time doing what you do best.

What Is a Professional Web Designer?

A professional web designer is someone who specializes in designing websites. This person has the expertise to create beautiful and functional websites, using visual and interactive design elements to draw in potential customers. Web designers create the aesthetics and overall look and feel of a website, while web developers build the functionality. A good web designer understands the importance of functionality but also knows how to create eye-catching visuals to draw in customers and keep them engaged.

1. Technical Knowledge, Experience, and Industry Know-How

Technological changes occur regularly, and web designers need to stay up-to-date on the latest trends. A professional web designer is current on the latest tools and technologies — and understands how all of those pieces fit together to create an effective website.

When you hire a web designer, you can rely on their knowledge and experience to create a website that is user-friendly and easy to navigate. They'll also make sure the website is search-engine friendly and optimized for the best possible rankings. A professional web designer has likely worked with clients in your industry and has seen what works — and what doesn't.

2. Optimizing for Search Rankings and Mobile-Friendliness

These days, people turn to the internet to make purchases and find the information they need. To ensure your website is easy to find, your design must be optimized for search engine crawlers. A professional web designer knows how to create a website that is mobile-friendly, ensuring it looks great on any device.

Statistics show that more than half of all internet users access the web on mobile devices. Your website must be accessible on all of them — and a professional knows how to make that happen.

3. Meeting the Latest Web Standards

As your business grows, you'll likely want to update your website, adding new pages and changing visual elements. When you hire a professional web designer, they'll build your site using the latest web standards to ensure future updates are easy.

A professional web designer will design your website to function properly even after years of use. They'll make sure it's responsive, loads quickly, and looks great on all devices.

4. Designing an Attractive, User-Friendly Website

A professional web designer creates the look and feel of your website and knows what elements to include to make it engaging and user-friendly. They understand design and branding and how to incorporate these elements in a way that draws in customers while being easy to navigate.

When a potential customer visits your website, they should be able to easily move through the pages and know exactly what options are available to them. That clarity is something a professional designer builds in from the start.

5. Adhering to the Website's Purpose and Goals

You likely have specific goals for your website — but not all websites are created equally. Different types of websites are designed for different purposes, and a professional web designer understands those differences.

A professional will work with you to understand the purpose of your website and the goals you hope to achieve. They'll then create a design with the right structure and functionality to accomplish those goals — not just make something that looks nice.
