When you work with Origo, you're not getting handed off to an account manager. You're working directly with the people who do the work.

Co-Owner & Head Creative

Jon

Jon is the creative force behind Origo. He leads design across every project — websites, logos, brand identities — and brings the same level of care to client relationships and copywriting.

He's the one asking the questions nobody else thought to ask, finding the story underneath the brief, and making sure every detail earns its place.

Jon also handles business development and sales, building the kind of long-term client partnerships that Origo is known for. He doesn't just want to close a deal — he wants to see your business win.

Off the Clock

When Jon isn't designing or talking shop, you'll find him around a fire — smoking meat, passing a cigar, and good conversation with good people.

Co-Owner & Quality Assurance

Emily

Emily is the standard-keeper at Origo. She makes sure that what goes out the door is actually ready to go — that every detail has been checked, every element is consistent, and nothing slips through the cracks.

Her eye for quality is what separates a good project from a great one.

As co-owner, she's also a core part of the vision that drives Origo forward — someone who cares about doing things right, not just doing things fast.

Off the Clock
