The Challenge

Reimagining Redemption Hill's Digital Identity

Redemption Hill Church wanted to strengthen their online presence — but their existing brand and website weren't reflecting who they were or what they offered their community. They needed a refreshed identity and a site that could serve both regular members and newcomers looking to connect.

Our team collaborated with church leadership to revitalize their brand identity and enhance their digital footprint. We developed a distinctive logo, designed the website with intuitive navigation and engaging visuals, and provided reliable hosting to ensure the site performs well for their congregation day in and day out.

Logo Design

A mark built around mission

The logo design process began with in-depth conversations with church leaders to understand their core values and the message they wanted to carry into the community. Inspired by the concept of redemption and the church's welcoming atmosphere, we crafted a logo featuring a stylized hill and cross — symbolizing faith and renewal.

Clean lines and balanced proportions ensure the mark works across every medium, from the website and social media to printed bulletins and signage. The result is a distinctive, memorable emblem that reinforces Redemption Hill's identity within the community.

Visual Identity

Typography & Color

Typography: For headings, we selected Merriweather — known for its readability and classic elegance — to convey a sense of tradition and trust. For body text, Open Sans was chosen for its clean, modern appearance, enhancing readability and accessibility across devices.

Color Palette: A monochromatic grayscale scheme was carefully chosen to reflect strength, simplicity, and timelessness — emphasizing clarity, structure, and approachability, while maintaining a refined, modern look.

Outcomes

What changed for Redemption Hill Church

Stronger Brand Identity

A new logo captures the church's mission with a modern, timeless design. The refined palette and typography create a cohesive identity that works across digital and print.

Improved Website Experience

The redesigned site features clear structure and easy navigation — service times, events, and sermons are all easy to find, with a mobile-friendly design that serves both members and newcomers.

Increased Participation

More accessible information about church activities contributed to a rise in event attendance and community involvement.

Reliable Hosting

A secure, high-performance hosting solution ensures 99.9% uptime, fast loading speeds, and regular security updates — so the church can share its message without technical interruptions.
