The Challenge

Rebranding and Strategizing for a Fresh Online Presence

Groomer's Seafood had built a strong reputation for quality — but their digital presence wasn't keeping pace with the business they'd become. They needed an identity that felt as fresh and trustworthy as the product they deliver, along with a website that could properly serve both retail and wholesale customers.

Our team set out to give Groomer's Seafood a polished, contemporary identity that highlights their expertise and commitment to providing high-quality seafood. We crafted a modern, coastal-inspired design focusing on intuitive navigation and visually compelling storytelling to engage both audiences.

Our Approach

Coastal branding built to convert

We incorporated custom nautical graphics — wave patterns and stylized seafood icons — to subtly emphasize the brand's connection to the ocean. These elements, alongside a new maritime-inspired color palette, became integral to Groomer's refreshed identity.

We expanded the website to include strategically enhanced pages detailing the wholesale ordering process, real-time inventory updates, and product highlights. These new pages provide clear, structured information about ordering options, pricing, delivery schedules, and minimum order requirements — making it easier for wholesale clients and local buyers to place orders efficiently.

Visual Identity

Typography & Color

Typography: The website features Lato for headings, conveying a clean and professional aesthetic, while Roboto is used for body text to enhance readability and accessibility.

Color Palette: Chosen to reflect strength, tradition, and freshness — reinforcing their reputation as a trusted seafood provider. The combination of deep blues, rich reds, and neutral tones creates a visually appealing and recognizable identity.

SEO Results

Maximizing Visibility & Conversions

Before our SEO work, Groomer's Seafood had limited visibility in search engines — which meant fewer potential customers discovering their brand. By optimizing their content, improving website performance, and implementing structured data, we ensured their pages were indexed properly and appeared for relevant seafood-related searches.

We conducted extensive keyword research and strategically integrated the right terms into the site — moving 20 key seafood-related keywords from page 3 to page 1 on Google. Their most important terms now appear at the top of search results, driving highly targeted traffic from customers who are ready to buy.

We also optimized meta titles and descriptions to be more compelling — using action-driven language that emphasizes Groomer's quality offerings — boosting their click-through rate by 18%. On the technical side, we reduced page load times by 25% and migrated to a secure hosting platform with 99.9% uptime.

Outcomes

What changed for Groomer's Seafood

Streamlined wholesale and delivery information reduced customer inquiries and improved the ordering workflow.

Enhanced User Experience

Increased Sales

A clearer, more accessible ordering process contributed to a rise in wholesale and local delivery orders.

Scalability
