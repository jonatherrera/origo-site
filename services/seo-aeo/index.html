What Is AEO?

The next generation of search isn't a results page — it's an answer.

When someone types a question into ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google's AI Overview, they get a direct answer — not a list of blue links. The businesses that get cited in those answers aren't just lucky. They're optimized for it.

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) means structuring your content, authority signals, and brand presence so that AI models recognize you as a credible, citable source in your field. It's the new frontier of discoverability — and most businesses don't know it exists.

SEO & AEO FAQ

SEO & AEO Questions

What's the difference between SEO and AEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) focuses on ranking your business in traditional search engines like Google. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) focuses on getting your business cited by AI assistants like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews — and Origo includes both in every engagement.

How does Origo's SEO process work?

We start with a full audit of your current site and competitive landscape, then build a roadmap covering technical fixes, on-page optimization, content strategy, and AEO structuring. From there we execute monthly with transparent reporting on what we did and what it produced.

How long does SEO take to show results?

SEO typically starts showing real results around the 6-month mark, with stronger compounding gains in the months that follow. Our work with 360 Collision produced a 329.8% increase in keyword rankings, and Groomer's Seafood saw a 423.1% increase in active users.

Do I need a new website to do SEO with Origo?

Not always. We can work with your existing site if the foundation is solid, or recommend a rebuild if it's actively holding back your rankings. The audit makes that call clear.

Will Origo guarantee a #1 ranking?

No reputable SEO agency guarantees rankings, because no one controls Google's algorithm. What we do guarantee is a transparent process, monthly reporting, and proven methodology — and our case studies show the kind of results that consistent work produces.

Does Origo do local SEO?

Yes. Local SEO is a core part of what we do, including Google Business Profile optimization, local citations, review strategy, and location-specific content. Many of our clients serve specific cities or regions.

How is AEO different from regular SEO?

AEO requires structuring content so AI models can extract clear, citable answers — direct phrasing, defined entities, structured data, and authoritative formatting. Traditional SEO still matters, but AEO is becoming critical as more searches happen through AI assistants. See our FAQ on AI & Trust for more.
