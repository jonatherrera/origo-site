The Problem

A logo isn't a brand. And a bad brand costs you more than you think.

Most small businesses have a logo they've outgrown, colors they chose randomly, and no consistent way to present themselves. The result? They look like every other company in their industry — and prospects don't remember them.

  • Inconsistent visuals that look unprofessional across platforms
  • No clear brand voice — messaging that sounds generic or off
  • A logo that doesn't scale, print well, or work in different contexts
  • Prospects can't tell what makes you different from the competition
  • You're embarrassed to hand out your business card or share your website

Our Approach

A brand system built to communicate, not just decorate.

We start with strategy — who you are, who you serve, and what makes you different. Then we build a visual and verbal identity system that expresses that clearly and consistently across every touchpoint.

The goal isn't to make something pretty. The goal is to make something that works — that attracts the right clients, builds trust faster, and makes every piece of your marketing stronger.

How It Works

A clear process. No surprises.

Most branding projects take 3–5 weeks from kickoff to delivery.

1

Discovery Call

We start by learning your business, your goals, and your audience. No questionnaire — a real conversation.

2

Brand Strategy

We define your positioning, your voice, and the core idea your brand will be built around.

3

Visual Direction

We present 2 distinct creative directions for you to choose from — mood boards and initial concepts.

4

Design & Refinement

We build out your chosen direction and refine it with your feedback over 2 revision rounds.

5

Final Delivery

You receive your complete brand package — every file, every format, and a guide for how to use it.

Is This Right for You?

This is for you if…

Honest criteria. We'd rather tell you now than waste both our time.

Good fit

  • You're launching a new business and need a brand from scratch
  • Your existing brand no longer reflects where your business is
  • You want something that looks as good as your competitors — or better
  • You're ready to invest in your business's long-term perception
  • You want a team that listens before they design

Not the right fit

  • You need a logo done in 48 hours
  • You want to design it yourself and just need someone to "clean it up"
  • You're not open to strategic input — just execution
  • You're not sure branding is worth investing in

Branding FAQ

Branding Questions

What's included in an Origo branding project?

A full branding project includes brand strategy, logo design, color palette, typography system, and a brand guidelines document. Depending on scope, we can also include secondary marks, brand voice, photography direction, and launch assets.

Do I get a logo or a full brand identity?

You get a full brand identity system, not just a logo. A logo on its own can't carry a brand — you need a coordinated system of marks, colors, type, and voice that work together everywhere your business shows up.

How many logo concepts will I see?

We typically present a small number of refined directions rather than a large volume of rough concepts. Our process invests heavily in strategy upfront so the directions we present are already aligned with your business — making the decision easier, not harder.

What if I already have a logo I like?

We can refresh and extend an existing logo into a fuller identity system rather than starting from scratch. Many established businesses don't need a rebrand — they need their existing brand to work harder across more places.

Do I own the final brand files?

Yes. You own all final brand assets and receive them in editable source files plus production-ready exports. We deliver everything you need to hand off to printers, signage vendors, or future partners. If you're evaluating any branding agency — including us — read our guide: Six Questions to Ask Before Trusting an Agency With Your Brand →

Can Origo handle printed materials too?

Yes. While our focus is digital presence, we routinely design business cards, signage, and other print materials as extensions of the brand identities we build. We don't print in-house — we have partnerships with small-business print shops who handle production and ship directly to you.

How long does a branding project take?

Branding projects typically run 6 to 12 weeks from kickoff to final delivery, depending on the depth of strategy work and feedback turnaround. See our full FAQ for more on timelines.
