The Problem

A neglected website is a liability, not an asset.

Most small business owners built their website and then forgot about it. Plugins go out of date. Security vulnerabilities accumulate. Pages break. And every day your site underperforms is a day you're leaving leads on the table.

  • Outdated plugins and themes creating security vulnerabilities
  • Slow load speeds killing your SEO and conversion rate
  • No one monitoring uptime — your site goes down and you don't know
  • Small content changes that take weeks to get done because you're not a priority
  • No backup strategy — one bad update and everything is gone

Our Approach

We become your website team — without the overhead.

On a Concierge plan, Origo handles everything your website needs to stay healthy, fast, secure, and up to date. Monthly maintenance, content updates, monitoring, backups — all handled proactively.

You get a dedicated point of contact who knows your site, responds fast, and treats your business like it's their own. No tickets, no queues, no runaround.

Is This Right for You?

This is for you if…

The Concierge service works best for businesses with an established site they need to protect and grow.

Good fit

  • You have a live website that represents your business
  • You don't want to think about your website — you just want it to work
  • You're tired of chasing a developer for small changes
  • You want proactive management, not reactive fixes
  • You understand your website is a business tool that deserves care

Not the right fit

  • You don't have a website yet (check out Web Design first)
  • You want a developer on call 24/7 for custom builds
  • You need hourly development work, not a retainer
  • Your site is on a platform we can't access or manage
