Website Concierge FAQ

Website Concierge Questions

What is Website Concierge?

Website Concierge is Origo's ongoing website management service: a 12-month partnership where we handle updates, monitoring, security, performance, and content changes so you never have to think about your website. It's offered in three tiers based on how much support and capacity you need.

Why is Website Concierge a 12-month agreement?

Websites need consistent attention to perform, security patches, content updates, performance tuning, and SEO maintenance compound over time. A 12-month commitment lets us actually do the work that produces results, not just react to emergencies.

What's included in Website Concierge?

All tiers include hosting management, security monitoring, regular backups, performance optimization, and small content updates. Higher tiers add larger content updates, design changes, new pages, analytics reporting, and priority response times.

How fast does Origo respond to update requests?

Response times depend on your tier, ranging from same-week turnaround to priority same-day response. Emergencies, like security issues or site downtime, are addressed immediately regardless of tier.

What if I need a major change to my website?

Major changes like new sections, redesigns, or substantial new functionality are handled as separate scoped projects rather than concierge work. We'll quote those in advance so you always know what's covered and what isn't.

Do I have to use Website Concierge if Origo built my site?

No, but most clients choose to. The sites we build perform best when the team that built them keeps maintaining them, and concierge is significantly more cost-effective than rebuilding a neglected site later.
