The Problem

Most business copy sounds like it was written by a committee — or a robot.

Vague, jargon-heavy, or just forgettable. If your website copy doesn't grab attention, explain your value clearly, and tell the reader exactly what to do next — you're leaving money on the table.

  • Visitors land on your site and leave without understanding what you actually do
  • Your copy is a list of features when it should be answering "what's in it for me?"
  • You wrote it yourself and you know it's not working — but you don't know why
  • Generic AI-generated copy that sounds like every other site in your industry
  • No clear call to action — visitors don't know what to do next

Our Approach

Copy built on StoryBrand. Optimized for real humans.

We use the StoryBrand framework to structure your messaging so it's instantly clear: who you help, what problem you solve, and how to get started. No fluff, no filler.

Your client is the hero of the story. Origo positions you as the guide who helps them win. That shift in framing changes everything about how your copy reads — and how it converts.

Sample Deliverable — The Tatum Law Practice

Before

"Lisa Tatum founded The Tatum Law Practice in 2011. She is a former prosecutor and has served as President of the State Bar of Texas."

→

After

"You don't need a lawyer who treats you like a case number. You need someone who picks up the phone, explains what's actually happening, and helps you make decisions you feel confident about."

Before

"Our firm provides quality legal services with a commitment to client satisfaction."

→

After

"Big firms have the credentials but treat you like a number. Small firms are personal but lack firepower. We're the rare combination: former State Bar President, former prosecutor, 100+ trials, who still answers emails the same day."

Copywriting FAQ

Copywriting Questions

What kind of copy does Origo write?

Origo writes website copy, brand messaging, service-page content, email sequences, and long-form content like blog and thought-leadership pieces. Copywriting is led personally by Jon, our Head Creative and Co-Founder, so every word that goes out reflects the strategic and brand judgment that runs through the rest of our work.

What framework does Origo use for website copy?

We use the StoryBrand framework as our foundation, which positions your customer as the hero of the story and your business as the trusted guide. It produces copy that's clear, focused, and built around what your customer actually cares about.

Will my copy be written by a human or AI?

Your copy is written by Jon, our Head Creative. We use AI as a research and refinement tool — never as a replacement for the strategic judgment and voice that make copy actually work.

What if I want to write some of the copy myself?

That works too. We can collaborate on a hybrid approach where you provide raw content or drafts and we shape, edit, and structure them into final copy. Many founders prefer this for content that requires their personal voice.

Does copywriting come with website projects?

Copywriting is a separate service that can be bundled into a website project or purchased on its own. Bundling usually produces better results because copy and design are developed together rather than in sequence.

Can Origo write copy in my brand voice?

Yes. Brand voice development is part of our process — we capture how you actually talk and write so the final copy sounds like you, not like a generic agency. For ongoing work, we develop voice guidelines that keep everything consistent over time.
