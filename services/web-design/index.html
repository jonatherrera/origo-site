The Problem

Most small business websites don't convert. They just exist.

You've got a site. Maybe you built it yourself or hired someone cheap. But it doesn't rank, it doesn't inspire confidence, and it's not bringing in leads. It's just... there.

  • Slow load times that kill conversions before the page even loads
  • No clear messaging — visitors don't know what you do in 5 seconds
  • Looks nothing like your brand on desktop, tablet, or mobile
  • Hard to update — you're dependent on a developer for every small change
  • No strategy behind the layout or the copy

Our Approach

Strategy first. Design second. Always on brand.

We don't start with colors and layouts. We start by asking: what do you need this website to do? Then we build a site that does it — cleanly, quickly, and with a design that reflects who you actually are.

Every Origo website is hand-coded (no bloated page builders), mobile-first, and built with clear calls to action at every step.

Want us to keep it running too? Ask about our Website Concierge plan — monthly maintenance, updates, and monitoring so you never have to think about your site again.

How It Works

From kickoff to launch in 4–6 weeks.

A clear process keeps projects on track and avoids surprises.

1

Discovery & Strategy

We map out your sitemap, goals, target audience, and what success looks like after launch.

2

Wireframes

Low-fidelity layouts for key pages so we're aligned on structure before design begins.

3

Design

Full visual design — every page, every state, every breakpoint. Two revision rounds included.

4

Development

Clean, hand-coded HTML/CSS/JS (or your chosen CMS) built for speed and reliability.

5

Launch

Full QA, deployment to your host, DNS configuration, and a walkthrough of your new site.
